About

Deadpool, also known as Wade Wilson, first appeared in
*New Mutants #98* in 1990 as a mercenary with a darkly
humorous personality. After undergoing an experimental
procedure to cure his cancer, he gained a regenerative
healing factor but was left disfigured. Currently, Deadpool
continues his chaotic adventures as an anti-hero, often
breaking the fourth wall and engaging in outrageous antics.

Movies

DeadPool

Deadpool is a 2016 American black comedy superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. Directed by Tim Miller (in his directorial debut) and written by Rhett Reese and Paul Wernick, it is a spin-off of the X-Men film series and its overall eighth installment. The film stars Ryan Reynolds in the title role, alongside Morena Baccarin, Ed Skrein, T.J. Miller, Gina Carano, and Brianna Hildebrand. In the film, Wade Wilson hunts the man responsible for giving him mutant abilities and a scarred physical appearance, becoming the antihero Deadpool.

DeadPool 2

Deadpool 2 is a 2018 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Deadpool. It is the sequel to Deadpool (2016) and the eleventh installment in the X-Men film series. The film was directed by David Leitch and written by Rhett Reese, Paul Wernick, and Ryan Reynolds, who stars in the title role alongside Josh Brolin, Morena Baccarin, Julian Dennison, Zazie Beetz, T.J. Miller, Brianna Hildebrand, and Jack Kesy. In the film, Deadpool forms the X-Force to protect a young mutant from the time-traveling soldier Cable.

DeadPool & Wolverine

Deadpool & Wolverine is a 2024 American superhero comedy movie. It was released on July 26, 2024 in North America. It is based on the Marvel Comics character Deadpool. It is the sequel to Deadpool (2016) and Deadpool 2 (2018) and the 34th movie of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). It is the first Deadpool movie to be a part of the MCU. It was directed by Shawn Levy.

Weapons

Deadpool (Ryan Reynolds) can be seen dual-wielding IWI Desert Eagle Mark XIX pistols as his primary firearms, using them to great effect. His Desert Eagles are current-production Mark XIX models with over-barrel Picatinny-style rails made by IWI in Israel. They are recognizable by their safeties, which are retained from the original production Mark XIX; the U.S.-made Magnum Research version, in contrast, now has the "teardrop"-style safety found on the original Mark I.
Beretta 92FS Inox One of the patrons at the "Sister Margaret's Home for Wayward Girls" pulls a Beretta 92FS Inox when Weasel (T.J. Miller) gets threatened.
Beretta Px4 Storm One of the bar patrons at the "Sister Margaret's Home for Wayward Girls" pulls a Beretta Px4 Storm pistol.
Smith & Wesson Model 29 When Wade gathers up "all the guns", a Smith & Wesson Model 29 revolver with a 8 3/8" barrel is seen being put in a bag.
The katanas of Earth-12101's Deadpool are a
set of sharpened swords composed of Carbonadium capable of deflecting powerful attacks and impacts such as bullets or explosives. Carbonadium is an element which can disrupt the Healing Factor of Mutants.
When Wade asks for "all the guns", Weasel (T.J. Miller) first takes up a short-barreled Remington 870 shotgun fitted with a side folding stock. A second short-barreled 870 with front and rear pistol grips and a top folding stock is picked up by Wade. The short-barreled 870 handled by Weasel and seen in the gun bag appears to be an airsoft model, the plasticy finish, incorrect looking pump, the presences of a inner-barrel, and a tube mag support ring around a barrel this short gives this away as a airsoft replica.
The IMI Micro Uzi is prominently featured as a compact, powerful firearm. Chambered in 9x19mm, it offers a high rate of fire (up to 1,200 RPM) and is ideal for close-quarters combat.
"Blind Al" (Leslie Uggams) gives Deadpool a Colt Model 1908 Vest Pocket with pearl grips. Weasel (T.J. Miller) mentions that it's a .45 caliber, but in reality, the pistol is only a .25. Vanessa (Morena Baccarin) fires the same pistol in an extended scene.
